Feel yourself at home at Roasters Cafe

Our cozy and chill atmosphere is the ideal space to enjoy the best coffee and pastries.

Located in the vibrant neighborhood of the West Village in lower Manhattan, the exciting food scene can only inspire us to offer the most creative menu we can. Our pasion for good coffee and the world of baked goods is endless, that is why we think you will love our espresso and capuccino, along with our croissant and many other delicacies.

The menu is a highly curated sample of european cuisine

Our selection of dishes will most certainly surprise you.

We have a wide variety of options ranging from the light mediterranean cuisine to authentic nordic dishes. Our selection of sandwiches from the more traditional panini to our own creations await you.

For all the sweet tooth enthusiasts!

Enjoy french pâtisserie with our many options.

We dedicate a big part of the menu to our pastries. At Roasters Cafe you can find tartines, pain au chocolat, éclair, macarons, palmiers, mille-feuille and much more.
